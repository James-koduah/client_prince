About Us
Who Are We?

We are Clean Hands Painting And ArtWork Company

We are a business that has a focus on providing beauty in the Housing Industry. We are run by a dedicated team that has brought praise with each job completed. We have a base of operations in the Greater Accra Region which extends all througout the Volta, Eastern and Central Region.

Our Company was founded by our current CEO Prince Vigbedor to provide painting services to people, our services include the beautification of entire buildings, apartments, or just the need to add some colors to a place. We were founded in the year 2005 and have since been adding color to the world building after building.

What We Offer

We offer Exprienced and professional hands that will help you bring out the best in your project. From advice on picking your colors to the design that will be the most appealling, we will be with you every step of the way We ensure only the best of everything is used. We do not cut coners when it comes to the quality of our work. Throughout our dealings together, we assure you of quality. We provide estimates for every work that is to be done in respects to price and have gained the reputation of being an affordable service provider.

Our products and services include, Wall Treatment, Swahili Design, Interior And Exterior Design, Grafiato Design, Pop Design, Wall Skimming, Metallic Flooring, 3D Epoxy Floor Coating, CCTV Camera Installation, Wallpaper Installation Plastic Wallpaper.

Our Mission

We aim to become a household name for painting services and to be known for our quality. The dream is to expand our business opertions from the current geographic area we serve to all 16 regions of Ghana and in the long term expand internationally. We also aim to provide employment through our services to young and talented individuals that need an avenue to grow and develop their skills.

We also aim to add more departments to our slew of services. Our most current consideration is producing our own brand of paint and moving into the construction Industry by producing some building materials like blocks.
